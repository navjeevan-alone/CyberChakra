The Vortex

At Cyber Chakra, we stand at the cutting edge of cyber forensic innovation, boasting tools such as 'Vortex'. This state-of-the-art invention is revolutionizing digital forensic investigations by seamlessly automating the RAM dump collection and analysis process.

Our unwavering commitment to enhancing efficiency, reducing human error, and accelerating digital investigations epitomizes our dedication to excellence in the cyber forensics domain.

Clients

Recognized by

Forensic investigations

The proposed system can be utilized during digital forensic investigations to quickly and efficiently collect RAM dumps from various devices with different operating systems.

Malware analysis

Security professionals can use this approach to obtain memory dumps from potentially compromised systems to analyze and identify any malicious processes or software.

Debugging and troubleshooting

Developers can use this tool to collect memory dumps for debugging and troubleshooting software issues that are difficult to reproduce or diagnose.

System performance analysis

Administrators can obtain RAM dumps to analyze and optimize system performance by identifying memory leaks, bottlenecks, or other resource-consuming issues.

Incident response

In the event of a security breach or cyber attack, incident response teams can use this tool to quickly gather RAM data from affected systems to assess the impact and identify potential threats.
